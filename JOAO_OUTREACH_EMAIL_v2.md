## Why this version (deltas from v1)

- **Non-technical framing:** Explicit "I am not a technical person" upfront — removes any risk of him perceiving me as a peer who might extract knowledge and walk. Signals learner, not competitor.
- **India-only scope:** No Asia, no Middle East, no Asian Games framing. "For my own club to start with, later maybe a handful more." Reduces the perceived commercial threat.
- **No co-founder / equity / team invite:** Removes anything he might feel obligated to consider or refuse. Zero ask beyond the meeting itself.
- **Paid technical consultancy** mentioned only as *his call, later, if he wants* — not a proposal. Preserves the option without triggering a "what's your offer?" reflex.
- **No P.S., no pitch tail:** v1's WhatsApp-distribution P.S. was pitch-flavoured; removed.
- **Removed "expectations from the association" section:** Building expectations of my own is exactly the trap this rewrite is meant to avoid.
