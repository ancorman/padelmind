## Appendix — The technology in plain English

For technically-curious members of the committee:

- A single 4K outdoor camera, weather-rated, connects via PoE to a small computer in your network cupboard
- The computer buffers match recordings locally (so it works even if your WiFi has a brief outage), then uploads completed matches to our secure cloud
- In the cloud, computer vision models track every player, the ball, and where each shot lands. A trained AI classifier — designed in partnership with a senior padel coach — assigns a class and quality score to every shot
- The output is a structured per-player report delivered via WhatsApp
- The same pattern is used by professional sports broadcasters (Hawk-Eye, Wingfield, Clutch) — we're bringing it to recreational play at recreational prices
