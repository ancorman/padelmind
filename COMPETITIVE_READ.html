At parity — table stakes

  • Phone capture — everyone's here now
  • Heatmaps + match stats — commodity
  • Auto highlights — commodity
  • These get you in the game, not ahead

Behind — close these

  • Native app — rivals are native; you're a PWA. Fine for MVP, a gap long-term
  • Technique scoring — Padel AI already ships per-shot Playtomic scoring + drills. Your "moat" area — they're live, you're building
  • Rating scale — Playtomic is the standard; you need a stance on it

Genuinely different — lean hard

  • WhatsApp-native delivery — literally no competitor does this
  • India-first distribution (AutomationXpert muscle)
  • Human coach designing the rubric vs pure AI — if it's demonstrably better/explainable

The verdict

Portable + analytics is a race you won't win on features — SwingVision is more mature, and Padel AI already ships the technique scoring you consider your moat. If the pitch is "phone-based padel analysis," you're one of six, behind two.

Your only defensible edges are distribution and the human-coach rubric: WhatsApp-native reach into Indian clubs (nobody else), and a real coach's eye that AI-only rivals can't fake — if it produces feedback players trust more than an auto-score. Treat heatmaps/stats/highlights as table stakes to reach parity cheaply, ship the PWA MVP to prove the WhatsApp loop, and pour the real energy into the coach rubric + India go-to-market. That's the wedge — not the camera.
